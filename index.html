About Water Wastage

Water wastage in both domestic and agricultural sectors poses a critical threat to our environment and sustainability. In domestic settings, wastage often occurs due to leaky faucets, excessive use for non-essential purposes like car washing or watering lawns, and inefficient water fixtures. This results in a significant loss of a precious resource.
